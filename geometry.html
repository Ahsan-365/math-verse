The Tesseract (4D Cube)

A tesseract is the four-dimensional analogue of the cube. What you are seeing is a mathematically accurate projection from 4D to 3D, and then 3D to 2D canvas, rotating simultaneously across multiple 4D hyper-planes (XY, XZ, XW, YW).

Vertices: 16
Edges: 32
Faces: 24 (Squares)
Cells: 8 (Cubes)
