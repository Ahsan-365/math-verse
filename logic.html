Inference Rules Reference

Modus Ponens (MP)
p→q, p ⊢ q
Modus Tollens (MT)
p→q, ¬q ⊢ ¬p
Hypothetical Syll. (HS)
p→q, q→r ⊢ p→r
Disjunctive Syll. (DS)
p∨q, ¬p ⊢ q
Simplification
p∧q ⊢ p (or q)
Conjunction
p, q ⊢ p∧q
Double Negation
¬¬p ⊢ p
Contrapositive
p→q ⊢ ¬q→¬p
