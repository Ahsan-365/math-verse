Ramsey Theory

R(s,t): minimum n such that any 2-coloring of edges of Kₙ contains a red Kₛ or blue Kₜ. Known exact values are rare.

Key Results

  • • R(3,3) = 6: Any 6-person party has 3 mutual friends or 3 mutual strangers
  • • R(4,4) = 18 (Exact, proven 1979)
  • • R(5,5): Known to be between 43 and 48 — open problem!
  • • Upper bound: R(s,t) ≤ R(s-1,t) + R(s,t-1)
  • • Probabilistic lower bound: R(k,k) ≥ √2 · 2^(k/2)
